Cancelling your Membership

Recurring Payments will be deduceted automatically from your Bank Account. Despite of that, there is no contract. allowing you to cancel your membership anytime.

If you cancel your membership and rejoin the gym, you will be charged with the joining fee again. For this reason, it's better to pause your membership if you want to take a short break

Pausing your Membership

Pausing takes effect immediately, being the pause fee is 6 € per month

Once you un-pause your membership, you will be again charged your monthly suscription
